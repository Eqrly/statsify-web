Command Arguments Description Alliases
s!help [command] Gives a command list for the bot support
s!info Gives bot statistics and information on the creators ping, stats, uptime, data
s!links Gives the bot invite link and support server link. add, invite, invites
s!suggest (suggestion) Sends your suggestion straight to the developers, spamming this command will get you blacklisted
s!bug (bug or glitch) Sends your bug report straight to the developers, please provide as much information as possible. Spamming this command will get you blacklisted glitch
s!prefix (prefix for your server) Changes the default prefix of s! to the prefix provided by the user
s!color (hex color) Changes the color of certain embeds to the color proived. For Donators only. colors, colour, colours
s!verify (IGN) Adds your account to the database, You must link your discord account on mc.hypixel.net. Verifying allows for you to do quicker commands such as !bw with no other paramaters link, v, connect, register
s!unverify (IGN) Removes your account to the database, You have been previously verified unlink, uv, deconnect, unregister
